THE MARIO MOVIE



The Mario movie is an animated film directed by Aaron Horvath and Micheal Jelenic.


The Plot

The Plot follows the life of Mario, who with his brother, Luigi run a failing plumbing company, but whose lives turn around when they enter a magical pipe leading to the Mushroom Kingdom.

We also meet an electric and eccentric cast of characters, like Princess Peach, Donkey Kong, among other characters
